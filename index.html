Calendary is an intuitive website designed for individuals and teams looking to streamline their scheduling and task management processes. Users can easily view their schedules in various formats such as monthly, weekly, or daily views. They can add or edit tasks/events with ease. The platform also offers a dedicated help page for user assistance and furthermore it offers an 'About' webpage specified to tell users more about our Calendary's mission and functionalities.
